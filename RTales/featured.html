Tangy Potato Sabji

A tangy stir fry potatoes just gives that extra kick to plain rice or porridge. It does pair well with Khichdi, parathas or even a whole meal bread


Servings:4
Cooking time: 17minutes
Ingredients
    15g oil
  • 1 dry red chilli
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 65g Onion
  • ¼ tsp mustard powder
  • Pinch of Asafoetida
  • 200g potatoes diced (6cm)
  • ⅓ tsp turmeric powder
  • 6g Salt
  • 150g water
  • 150g tomatoes (quartered)
  • 1 tsp Coriander and Jeera powder
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • ½ tsp raw sugar
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Preparation
  • 1. Place oil and cinnamon in mixing bowl. Heat:3min/120C/spoon.
  • 2. Add ground mustard,onions and a pinch of asafeotida. Saute:
  • 4 min/120C/spoon
  • 3. Add potatoes, turmeric,salt and water. Cook: 6min/Varoma/spoon
  • 4. Add tomatoes,coriander and jeera powder, chilli powder and sugar
  • 5. Cook: 10 min/120C/spoon
  • 6. Transfer to serving bowl.
  • 7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
    • Variation: Add frozen peas(50g)at step 4.

Caramelised Pudding - South Indian Style

Desserts made for celebration an occasion in every south indian family... Caramelised Payasam (Caramelised Rice Pudding) is a favourite sweet.

Ingredients
    Garnishing
  • 12g ghee
  • 5g sg sliced almonds
  • 5g cashew nuts
  • 5g raisins
    Caramelized Milk
  • 100g Brown sugar
  • 80g double cream
  • 100g milk
    Rice pudding
  • 50g Basmati rice
  • 250g water
  • ¼ tsp Cardamom powder
    Preparation
  • 1. Place a simmering basket and weigh rice. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  • 2. Prepare garnishing: Add ghee and heat:1min/120C/spoon.
  • 3. Add sliced almonds, cashew nuts and raisins.
  • 4. Saute: 8mins/120C/spoon. Transfer to a bowl.
  • 5. To caramelise sugar: Place sugar and cream in mixing bowl. Heat: 8min/120C/speed1.
  • 6. Add 100g milk, through the lid hole,cover with MC and mix well: 30sec/speed1. Transfer to a bowl.
  • 7. Boil 250gwater on Kettle mode/100C and transfer to a jar.
    • Reserve. Rinse mixing bowl with cold water. Place drained rice in the mixing bowl. Cover with lid and measuring cup. Grind: turbo 2 seconds and scrape down the sides, cover and Turbo again. Scrape down the sides of the mixing bowl and lid.
  • 8. Add reserved hot water to the mixing bowl. Cover with lid.
  • 9. Cook for 10 min/Varoma/reverse speed1
  • 10. Add Caramelized milk and cook for 5 min/Varoma/reverse speed 1
  • 11. Transfer to serving bowl and garnish the roasted nuts and raisins.
