Sue Patel, the face of The Recipe Tales, was inspired by the struggles that those with food allergies go through in order to cook food that adheres to their needs. Having experienced firsthand how most recipe blogs are seldom inclusive of these needs, she decided to channel her unique knowledge and passion for cooking into creating a platform where even those with dietary restrictions can enjoy simple homemade versions of popular dishes.

For Sue, food is not only something that one makes simply to sustain themself, but also an enjoyable activity to share with everyone. She seeks to make cooking easy and fun but also something to look forward to after a long day. Understanding that food should be pleasureable for as many people as possible no matter their background or experiences is an important part of The Recipe Tales.

Sue wishes everyone well on their culinary journey with the simple maxim: "Simple, Safe, Scrumptious!"
